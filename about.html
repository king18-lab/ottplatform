FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Stackly OTT

What is Stackly OTT?
Stackly OTT is a premium streaming service offering thousands of movies, live sports, kids content, and original series across multiple languages including English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, and more.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime with no hidden fees or penalties. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
What devices are supported?
Stackly works on smartphones (iOS & Android), tablets, laptops, smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Android TV), streaming sticks (Firestick, Roku, Chromecast), and gaming consoles.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, new users get a 7-day free trial on any plan. No payment required upfront. Cancel anytime during the trial.
Is there regional content available?
Absolutely! We offer content in Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Hindi, Bengali, Gujarati, Marathi, and English. New regional titles added every week.
How many devices can stream simultaneously?
Basic plan: 1 device · Premium plan: 4 devices · Family plan: 6 devices. You can also download content on mobile devices for offline viewing.
